Our Tea Collection

MASALA CHAI

Masala chai, also known as spiced tea, is a popular Indian beverage enjoyed for its rich flavor and comforting aroma. It's a warm hug in a cup, perfect for chilly mornings or a cozy evening pick-me-up. A classic and robust tea with a bold flavor.

GREEN TEA

Unveiling the Power of Green Tea: A Refreshing Journey for Mind and Body.A light and refreshing tea known for its health benefits.

CARDOMOM TEA

Cardamom, often referred to as the "queen of spices," is a pod-shaped seed native to South Asia. Green cardamom, the most commonly used variety for tea, possesses a unique flavor profile. It's a captivating blend of citrusy notes, a touch of sweetness, and a hint of warmth that lingers on the palate.

GINGER TEA

Ginger tea, a simple yet powerful beverage, has been enjoyed for centuries in various cultures around the world. Made by steeping ginger root in hot water, it boasts a warm, slightly spicy flavor and a comforting aroma. But beyond its taste, ginger tea is lauded for its potential health benefits. Let's delve deeper into this remarkable drink.

PERFECT BLEND

A perfect blend transcends simply combining different types of tea leaves. It's a symphony of flavors, aromas, and textures that come together to create a unique and harmonious experience.
